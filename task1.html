About us

SBRR Mahajana First Grade College is at an epochal moment in its cherished history, since it was founded in 1982, born as an offspring of Mahajana Education Society (1937). It started as a Womenâ€™s College offering B.A and B.Com programmes and was admitted to the Grant-in-aid scheme in the year 1987, and in 1988 became a co-educational institution. As the college made rapid strides, being committed to academic excellence and qualitative development, B.Sc. programme was introduced in the year 1988, BBM programme was started in 1994, followed by Biotechnology, Microbiology, Biochemistry combinations in B.Sc. in 2004. The BCA programme was introduced in 2008.

From a modest beginning the college has today branched out into a well reputed institution with its multi-dimensional growth. As a logical perseverance of its primary desire, on the threshold of the 21st Century, the college started PG programmes in 1999 from a new campus situated five kms. away from the main Campus. The institution now offers post-graduate degree and diploma courses in thirteen disciplines, which include Computer Application, Biotechnology, Microbiology, Biochemistry, Computer Science, Information Technology, Business Administration, Commerce, Social Work, Chemistry, Economics and Tourism Administration. It is poised to start more number of new programmes in the years to come. It is granted Community College under the UGC Scheme, offering Diploma in Hospitality & Catering and Diploma in Museology. This programme is meant for the general public as well as for the interested students of the college.
